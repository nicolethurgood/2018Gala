GALA

Nov. 17 2017

About

Come out and enjoy a formal night to celebrate your accomplishments with your fellow classmates. Create a few last memories over food, drinks, and entertainment! Sign up and don't miss out on this unforgettable event! This is a formal event so students are encouraged to dress up. A cash-only bar will be available to those with a proper ID. Transportation will be available with a student ID at Gleason Circle starting at 6:00pm and lasting all night.Tickets are $10 for RIT seniors and $15 for guests. For more information, please see the FAQ page. If you have any additional questions, feel free to stop by the CAB office at 03-A740 or email cab.rit.edu.
